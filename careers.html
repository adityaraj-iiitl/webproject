Our mission is to make the job-application journey stress-free by giving users a clean dashboard, reminders, analytics, and a complete tracking system.

To make this possible, we are looking for passionate learners and developers who want to grow with us.

Working at JobTracker gives you the opportunity to:

Work with modern technologies (React, Firebase, Node.js, APIs)

Learn UI/UX design and dashboard creation

Collaborate in a fast-moving student-driven environment

Contribute ideas and see them implemented immediately

Gain portfolio-worthy experience for internships and placements
