Job Hunt Pro Launches Advanced Job Tracking Platform for Students and Professionals

Revolutionary Dashboard set to Transform the Job Application Experience


Lucknow, UP, India — March 2025 — Job Hunt Pro, an innovative platform designed for job seekers, today announced the release of its new Job Tracking System, created to simplify and organize the entire job-search journey.

With students applying to dozens of companies during placements, and professionals managing multiple opportunities simultaneously, keeping track of deadlines, interviews, follow-up dates, and application statuses has become increasingly challenging. Job Hunt Pro
addresses this problem by offering a centralized, intuitive, and user-friendly dashboard.

"Our goal is to make the job-search process stress-free and organized," said Aarav Reddy, Product Lead at Job Hunt Pro. "This platform gives users clarity, structure, and full control over their job applications so they never miss important opportunities again."



Key Features of Job Hunt Pro


The platform includes a variety of tools tailored to support job seekers at every stage:

Complete Application Tracking (Applied, Interview Scheduled, Selected, Rejected)

Smart Deadline & Interview Reminders

Company Notes & Information Storage

Resume Upload and Document Management

Progress Analytics Dashboard

Cross-device Syncing & Secure Account Login

These features make Job Hunt Pro an essential tool for students preparing for campus placements, fresh graduates entering the workforce, and professionals seeking better opportunities.



Availability


The Job Hunt Pro web application is now available for use globally.
The Android mobile app will be released soon, enabling users to track applications on the go. Early users will receive priority access and feature previews.
