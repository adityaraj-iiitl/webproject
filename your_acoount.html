YOUR ACCOUNT

        Your Job Hunt Pro account is your personal space to manage, track, and organize every part of your job-search journey. With a secure login and synchronized data across devices,

 What You Can Do With Your Account


1. Add & Manage Job Applications


Easily create entries for each company you apply to, including:

Job title

Company name

Application date

Deadline

Status (Applied, Interview Scheduled, Selected, Rejected)

Notes and follow-up plans

Your account keeps everything neatly organized.



2. Track Your Progress


Your account helps you visualize your job journey with:

Number of applications submitted

Interviews scheduled

Rejections and selections

Pending actions

This makes it easier to plan your next steps.



3. Set Reminders & Alerts


Never miss:

Interview dates

Follow-up reminders

Application deadlines

Your Job Hunt Pro account ensures you stay on track.



4. Save Important Documents


Upload and securely store:

Resumes

Cover letters

Offer letters

Company notes

Your files remain easily accessible whenever needed.



5. Access Your Data Anywhere


Your account works across:

Laptop

Desktop

Mobile browser

Your data syncs automatically, so you can continue from any device.
